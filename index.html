Interpretation

All the explications about this graphics and the other not presented here, are detailled in the Read.me The objective of this graph is to compare clustering methods on meteorological data.

Discussion

The first challenge was about finding datas with enough criteria with a high number of locations in France. The second main step was to transform data and compute clustering and PCA. Plotting the data with Matplotlib and Plotly (available in the Jupyter Notebook) were 2 ways to present clusters.
